Help

Piece placement

  • Click on an element of the palette to select it.
  • Click on the playfield to place that element. Some elements ("decoration") are drawn as overlay
  • Cliking on the playfield while the "Active ?" checkbox is ticked place a unique (per playfield) active piece. It can then be moved at will in the Field manipulation area.
  • Checking "Advance after click" will do the same as clicking on the "New copy" button.
  • Rectangular fill fills a rectangular area of the selected element. The area is determined by two clicks.
  • Recursive fill works like the paint bucked of your average image editor.

Diagram exportation and importation

  • Clicking on the export button will produce a string in the appropriate console. The string represents your drawn playfield in an encoded form.
  • You can select which part of the diagram you wish to encode (the whole playfield or only the current frame.
  • You can select whetever you want pure code, an already BBtagged code or the direct links to either the viewer or the editor.
